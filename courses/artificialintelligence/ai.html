Course Objectives

Level 1

Philosophy of Artificial Intelligence & Foundational Knowledge

  • A deep understanding of the philosophy of AI, including but not limited to the works of Alan Turing, Geoffrey Hinton, Yann LeCun, Demis Hassabis, and other leading AI scientists and philosophers.
  • A deep understanding of how Large Language Models work and how to leverage them to create software that solves real-world problems.

Artificial Intelligence Application Development & Business Integration

  • How to develop cross-domain, full-scale AI applications that solve real business needs.
  • How to take real-life scenarios and understand how AI can be used to generate dollar value.

Capstone Project & Certification

  • AI Level 1 students will take the Capstone project in Semester 3.
  • As part of the Capstone project, students will build an end-to-end AI application to meet a real-world need and publish their application for general use.
  • At the end of the Capstone project, students will receive a Certificate in Applied Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Career Preparation & Professional Growth

  • After the Capstone, students will be provided guidance on resume building, profile building, and job applications.
  • Students will be able to tackle real-world problems and work with professional teams to develop advanced AI applications.

Level 2

Advanced Model Development & Research

  • Ability to develop Large Language and Large Vision models from scratch using open-source data sources.
  • Perform original research in the field of AI and develop new learning algorithms or improve upon existing strategies.

Complex Problem-Solving & Enterprise AI

  • Tackle complicated real-world scenarios that require original thought and innovation.
  • Manage enterprise-level AI applications at scale using MLOps and LLMOps.

Ethical AI & Responsible Development

  • Learn about the ethical considerations for AI applications, including data security, data privacy, and other factors.

Course Timing and Details

  • One class per week.
  • 60 minutes per class.
  • Each class will include a practical session (30 minutes), where students will build applications in every single class.
  • It is highly recommended to attend the class on a laptop or tablet and have a browser installed such as Google Chrome, Brave, Edge etc.
  • Each class will also include 30 minutes of lecture on theoretical concepts of AI.
